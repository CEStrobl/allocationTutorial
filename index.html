MC Modded RAM Allocation Tutorial

in only 4 steps!




Step 1 - Go to Profile Options

Go to the modpack and click on the three dots next to the play button. Then click on profile options.

Step 2 - Uncheck The Box

Uncheck the box that says Use System Memory Settings

Step 3 - Adjust Accordingly

Move the slider to about the 2/3's spot.

If your computer struggles with running modded minecraft, try moving the slider to around the 3/4's spot.

Step 4 - Enjoy You Better FPS :D

Now enjoy your wonderful abundance of fps :DDDDDDDDDD
